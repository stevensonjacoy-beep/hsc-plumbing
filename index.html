Service & Repair Work

Leak detection and repair, fixture replacements, pipe repairs, pressure issues — fast, reliable service work by professionals who diagnose the root cause, not just the symptom.

  • Leak detection & repair
  • Pipe & fitting repair
  • Faucet & fixture replacement
  • Pressure & flow issues

Drain Systems

New drain rough-ins, drain repairs, and complete waste system installations. We cut concrete, install ABS drains, bed in gravel, and leave it ready for inspection.

  • Concrete cutting & drain rough-in
  • ABS waste pipe installation
  • Floor drain installation
  • Drain repair & re-routing

Water Supply Systems

New water supply rough-in using PEX and copper, hot and cold distribution, manifold systems with individual shutoffs, and proper labelling that inspectors expect.

  • PEX & copper supply lines
  • Manifold & zone valves
  • Hot/cold water separation
  • Water filtration hookups

Emergency Repairs

Burst pipes, sudden leaks, no water — we respond quickly because water damage compounds fast. Call us and get a licensed plumber, not a dispatcher.

  • Burst & frozen pipe repair
  • Active leak response
  • Shut-off valve replacement
  • Water heater failures

Why Choose HSC

Licensed. Insured. Inspection‑Ready.

When you hire HSC Plumbing, you're hiring Red Seal journeyman plumbers — the highest recognized plumbing certification in Canada. Our work meets Ontario Building Code requirements and passes city inspections because it's done correctly, not just quickly.
